Fungible?
- Asset that can be exchanged with another asset is called as Fungible
- Eg: I can exchange 5 kg potatoes for 100 Rs note (Their price)

Non Fungible?
- Things that are same but cannot be exchanged
Eg: You cannot exchange two cars of different brands
- Assets where you cannot create a definite value for
